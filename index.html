what are crime series?

Crime series are diverse tv series with a genre of film that focus on crime. The stylistic approach to a crime serie varies from realistic portrayals of real-life criminal figures, to the far-fetched evil doings of imaginary arch-villains.

They can also be under the genre of crime drama, which are a types of drama shows which has as its primary focus or heroes people whose main occupation is to investigate, punish or commit crimesâ€”especially homicide, assault, robbery or racketeering. Notable crime dramas in TV history include Dragnet, Hill Street Blues, Homicide: Life on the Street, NYPD Blue, Law & Order, The Sopranos and CSI.
